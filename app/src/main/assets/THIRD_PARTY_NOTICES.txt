Ojas rPPG reference
Source: https://github.com/namdpran8/Ojas
License: MIT

GlucoSense adapts the documented Ojas signal-processing approach for this app's
camera pulse monitor: centered signal buffer, Hamming window, 45-200 BPM
frequency-domain search, SNR validation, previous-heart-rate lock, and
exponential smoothing. The full upstream Ojas app also uses MediaPipe,
native C++/Neon, KissFFT, and TensorFlow Lite assets.
